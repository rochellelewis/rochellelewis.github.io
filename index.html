About Me

I'm a full stack web developer, former coding bootcamp instructor, and recent graduate of the SANS/ICMCP Diversity Cyber Academy with over 10 years experience working in development. I'm a Certified Web Application Penetration Tester (GWAPT), Certified Incident Handler (GCIH), and a member of the GIAC Advisory Board.

I am a passionate supporter of progressive top-tier organizations, projects, and initiatives dedicated to serving the greater public good. I contribute my skills and talents to human-centered environments that thrive on positivity, enthusiasm, and joy. I believe relationships and communication are intentional practices. In this field that means mindfulness regarding the relationships between team members, end users, society and tech are key. Everyone should feel really good about the work that they do every day.

People > products, all day everyday.
